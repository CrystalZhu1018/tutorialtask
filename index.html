Infrastructure of Wellington

Internet infrastructure includes cables, antenna, radio, softwares and so on.
The infrastructure in this website is cameras, applications and terminals.
Those three categories are the most common things that people use every single day,
so we are able to understand that how important the internet is nowadays.

Next

Cameras

There are many different cameras around Wellington city to make sure the security of every citizens.

The three examples in this website are CCTV Surveillance Cameras in the city, Speed Cameras on the highway and Private Cameras at home.

There are some differences between their working process, but they all need Internet for their normal operation. The differences are how they connect or work with the Internet.

Next

Terminals

Terminals are the closest things to every single person that we use them directly.

The three examples here are PCs, laptops and smartphones which are the three most common devices. They have to connect to the Internet for normal operation, but some of them are wireless, some of them need cables.

Applications

Only with the terminals, what can be done is very limited. Connecting to the Internet is so that these applications can be used.

There are so many different applications nowadays. People can pay the fee, communicate with each other, watch videos, play games and so on. The included examples are Alipay whihc is for payment, Tiktok which represents social media and VPN.
